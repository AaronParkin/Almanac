Dogs
Starting with one of my favourite animals the Dog is capable of doing many things like barking and eating and chewing things you don't want them to and doing their business inside the house.
But despite all that they can be kind and caring creatures that if looked after properly can make great companions.
Overall I'd give Dogs an 8 on the hugibilty scale.
