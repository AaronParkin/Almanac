Fish

Fish are one of the best pets to begin with as they require little to no interaction, all a fish requires is a fish tank of appropriate size and clean water that is regularly cleaned to prevent the fish from dying. Feed the fish at regular intervals with fish food that you ca aquire from the local pet store. Make sure you do not overfeed the fish as this will also kill the fish.
